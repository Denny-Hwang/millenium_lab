## 4. Prior results to know about

TODO:

- Leray (1934): existence of weak solutions; uniqueness and
  regularity left open.
- Caffarelli–Kohn–Nirenberg (1982): partial regularity of weak
  solutions; the singular set has 1-dimensional Hausdorff
  measure zero.
- Beale–Kato–Majda (1984): regularity criterion in terms of the
  vorticity.
- Recent: non-uniqueness of weak solutions (Buckmaster–Vicol
  2019) — important caveat for what "the" solution means.
