## 4. Prior results to know about

TODO:

- Hadamard / de la Vallée Poussin (1896): no zeros on
  $\Re(s) = 1$ (giving the Prime Number Theorem).
- Hardy (1914): infinitely many zeros on the critical line.
- Selberg (1942): a positive proportion of zeros on the
  critical line.
- Conrey (1989): at least 40% of zeros on the critical line.
- Numerical verification: trillions of zeros computed, all on
  the critical line.
