# 04 — Yang–Mills Existence and Mass Gap

CMI official statement (gist): **For a compact simple gauge group, show
that quantum Yang–Mills theory exists on ℝ⁴ and has a positive mass
gap.**

This problem sits at the intersection of mathematical physics and
functional analysis. The repository tracks the following two directions
in parallel:

- Measure-theoretic construction (constructive QFT)
- Continuum limit from lattice gauge theory
